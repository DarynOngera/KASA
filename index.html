About KASA

Who We Are

The Kent African Student Association (KASA) is a vibrant community of African students and allies at Kent State University. We celebrate our rich heritage while building lasting friendships and creating unforgettable memories.

What We Do

We organize amazing parties, cultural events, educational workshops, and social gatherings. From traditional celebrations to modern parties, we bring the African community together and welcome everyone who wants to experience our culture.

Our Values

Unity, Culture, Excellence, and Fun! We believe in supporting each other academically and socially while staying connected to our roots and creating new traditions at Kent State.
