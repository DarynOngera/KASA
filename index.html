About KASA

Our Mission

The Kent African Student Association (KASA) is a vibrant community where we empower students to become confident leaders, celebrate their cultural identity, and contribute meaningfully to the Kent State community. We celebrate the richness of African cultures, empower student leaders, and create a home away from home for Africans and friends of Africa at Kent State University.

What We Do

Through cultural events, mentorship, advocacy, and collaboration, KASA strives to inspire pride, strengthen unity, and build bridges across diverse communities. We organize meaningful cultural celebrations, educational workshops, leadership development programs, and social gatherings that honor our heritage while fostering growth.

Our Impact

We empower students to become confident leaders, celebrate their cultural identity, and contribute meaningfully to the Kent State community. Through our initiatives, we create lasting connections, promote cultural understanding, and support academic and personal excellence among our members.
